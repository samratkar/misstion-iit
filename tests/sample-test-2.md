# Sample Test Paper 2: Atomic, Kinematics & Sets

**Time:** 90 Minutes | **Total Marks:** 100
**Instructions:** Correct: +4, Incorrect: -1.

---

### Physics (Kinematics)
1. A projectile is thrown with velocity $u$ at an angle $\theta$. At the highest point, its velocity is:
   A) $u \sin \theta$
   B) $u \cos \theta$
   C) $0$
   D) $u$

2. A man can swim in still water at $5 \text{ km/h}$. He wants to cross a river $1 \text{ km}$ wide flowing at $3 \text{ km/h}$ in the shortest possible time. The time taken is:
   A) 12 min
   B) 15 min
   /C) 20 min
   D) 10 min

---

### Chemistry (Atomic Structure)
3. The ratio of the radius of the $2^{nd}$ orbit of $Li^{2+}$ to the $3^{rd}$ orbit of $He^{+}$ is:
   A) $8:27$
   B) $4:9$
   C) $27:8$
   D) $1:2$

4. The wavelength of the first line of the Lyman series in Hydrogen spectrum is (R = Rydberg constant):
   A) $4/3R$
   B) $3R/4$
   C) $1/R$
   D) $4R/3$

---

### Mathematics (Sets & Relations)
5. If $A$ and $B$ are two sets such that $n(A) = 17, n(B) = 23$ and $n(A \cup B) = 38$, then $n(A \cap B)$ is:
   A) 4
   B) 2
   C) 6
   D) 0

6. Let $R$ be a relation on the set $\mathbb{N}$ defined by $xRy$ if $x+2y = 8$. The domain of $R$ is:
   A) $\{2, 4, 6\}$
   B) $\{1, 2, 3, 4\}$
   C) $\{2, 4, 8\}$
   D) $\{4, 8\}$

---

### Challenge Question (Combined)
7. An electron in a hydrogen-like atom (atomic number $Z$) is in an excited state with energy $-3.4 \text{ eV}$. If the De-Broglie wavelength of this electron is $\lambda$, and the radius of the orbit is $r$, show that the number of wavelengths that fit in the circumference is exactly the principal quantum number $n$.
