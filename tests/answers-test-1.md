# Answer Key & Explanations: Sample Test 1

## Physics
1. **Answer: A ($\pi/2$)**
   - Distance = Half circumference = $\pi R$.
   - Displacement = Diameter = $2R$.
   - Ratio = $\pi R / 2R = \pi/2$.

2. **Answer: A ($1 \text{ m/s}^2$)**
   - Acceleration is the slope of $v-t$ graph.
   - $a = \tan(45^\circ) = 1$.

## Chemistry
3. **Answer: B ($1.806 \times 10^{23}$)**
   - Moles of gas = 0.1.
   - Molecules of gas = $0.1 \times N_A = 6.02 \times 10^{22}$.
   - Atoms in triatomic gas = $3 \times 6.02 \times 10^{22} = 1.806 \times 10^{23}$.

4. **Answer: A (50 g)**
   - $CaCO_3 \rightarrow CaO + CO_2$.
   - 1 mole of $CaCO_3$ (100g) produces 22.4 L of $CO_2$.
   - To produce 11.2 L (0.5 mole), we need 0.5 mole of $CaCO_3 = 50$ g.

## Mathematics
5. **Answer: B ($2p^2 = 9q$)**
   - Roots: $\alpha, 2\alpha$.
   - Sum: $\alpha + 2\alpha = p \Rightarrow 3\alpha = p \Rightarrow \alpha = p/3$.
   - Product: $\alpha(2\alpha) = q \Rightarrow 2\alpha^2 = q \Rightarrow 2(p/3)^2 = q \Rightarrow 2p^2/9 = q \Rightarrow 2p^2 = 9q$.

6. **Answer: B ($\pm 4$)**
   - For equal roots, $D = b^2 - 4ac = 0$.
   - $(-k)^2 - 4(1)(4) = 0 \Rightarrow k^2 = 16 \Rightarrow k = \pm 4$.
