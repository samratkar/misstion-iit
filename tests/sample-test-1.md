# Sample Test Paper 1: Concept Foundation

**Time:** 60 Minutes | **Total Marks:** 60
**Instructions:** Each question carries 4 marks. -1 for incorrect answers.

---

### Section A: Physics (Kinematics)
1.  A particle moves in a circle of radius $R$. After completing half a revolution, the ratio of distance to displacement is:
    A) $\pi/2$
    B) $2/\pi$
    C) $\pi$
    D) $1$

2.  The velocity-time graph of a body is a straight line passing through the origin and making an angle of $45^\circ$ with the time axis. The acceleration of the body is:
    A) $1 \text{ m/s}^2$
    B) $0.5 \text{ m/s}^2$
    C) $\sqrt{2} \text{ m/s}^2$
    D) Zero

---

### Section B: Chemistry (Mole Concept)
3.  The number of atoms in 0.1 mole of a triatomic gas is: ($N_A = 6.02 \times 10^{23}$)
    A) $6.02 \times 10^{22}$
    B) $1.806 \times 10^{23}$
    C) $3.6 \times 10^{23}$
    D) $6.02 \times 10^{23}$

4.  How many grams of $CaCO_3$ are required to produce 11.2 L of $CO_2$ at STP?
    A) 50 g
    B) 100 g
    C) 25 g
    D) 200 g

---

### Section C: Mathematics (Quadratic Equations)
5.  If one root of $x^2 - px + q = 0$ is twice the other, then:
    A) $p^2 = 9q$
    B) $2p^2 = 9q$
    C) $p^2 = 2q$
    D) $9p^2 = 2q$

6.  The value of $k$ for which the equation $x^2 - kx + 4 = 0$ has equal roots is:
    A) $\pm 2$
    B) $\pm 4$
    C) $0$
    D) $16$
