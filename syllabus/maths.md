# Mathematics Syllabus - IIT JEE

## Algebra
1.  **Sets, Relations, and Functions.**
2.  **Complex Numbers and Quadratic Equations.**
3.  **Matrices and Determinants.**
4.  **Permutations and Combinations.**
5.  **Mathematical Induction.**
6.  **Binomial Theorem and its Simple Applications.**
7.  **Sequences and Series:** AP, GP, Special Series.

## Trigonometry
1.  **Trigonometric Functions and Identities.**
2.  **Inverse Trigonometric Functions.**

## Coordinate Geometry
1.  **Straight Lines.**
2.  **Conic Sections:** Circle, Parabola, Ellipse, Hyperbola.
3.  **Three Dimensional Geometry.**

## Calculus
1.  **Limits, Continuity, and Differentiability.**
2.  **Applications of Derivatives:** Tangents, Normals, Monotonicity, Maxima and Minima.
3.  **Integral Calculus:** Indefinite and Definite Integrals.
4.  **Applications of Integrals:** Area under curves.
5.  **Differential Equations.**

## Statistics and Probability
1.  **Statistics:** Measures of Dispersion.
2.  **Probability:** Classical, Addition/Multiplication Rules, Bayes Theorem.

## Mathematical Reasoning
1.  **Mathematical Logic and Statements.**
