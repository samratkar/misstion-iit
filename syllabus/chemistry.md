# Chemistry Syllabus - IIT JEE

## Physical Chemistry
1.  **Some Basic Concepts in Chemistry:** Mole Concept, Stoichiometry.
2.  **States of Matter:** Gaseous and Liquid States.
3.  **Atomic Structure:** Quantum Numbers, Bohr's Model, Electronic Configuration.
4.  **Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure:** VSEPR Theory, Hybridization, MOT.
5.  **Chemical Thermodynamics:** First and Second Laws, Enthalpy, Entropy, Gibbs Free Energy.
6.  **Solutions:** Raoult's Law, Colligative Properties.
7.  **Equilibrium:** Chemical and Ionic Equilibrium.
8.  **Redox Reactions and Electrochemistry:** Oxidation State, Electrochemical Cells, Nernst Equation.
9.  **Chemical Kinetics:** Rate of Reaction, Order, Arrhenius Equation.
10. **Surface Chemistry:** Adsorption, Colloids.

## Inorganic Chemistry
1.  **Classification of Elements and Periodicity:** Trends in Properties.
2.  **General Principles and Processes of Isolation of Metals:** Metallurgy.
3.  **Hydrogen and s-Block Elements.**
4.  **p-Block Elements:** Groups 13 to 18.
5.  **d and f Block Elements:** Coordination Compounds.
6.  **Environmental Chemistry.**

## Organic Chemistry
1.  **Purification and Characterization of Organic Compounds.**
2.  **Some Basic Principles of Organic Chemistry:** IUPAC, Isomerism, Electronic Effects.
3.  **Hydrocarbons:** Alkanes, Alkenes, Alkynes, Aromatic Hydrocarbons.
4.  **Organic Compounds Containing Halogens:** Haloalkanes and Haloarenes.
5.  **Organic Compounds Containing Oxygen:** Alcohols, Phenols, Ethers, Aldehydes, Ketones, Carboxylic Acids.
6.  **Organic Compounds Containing Nitrogen:** Amines, Diazonium Salts.
7.  **Polymers and Biomolecules.**
8.  **Chemistry in Everyday Life.**
