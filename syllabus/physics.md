# Physics Syllabus - IIT JEE

## Class 11
1.  **Units and Measurements:** Errors, Dimensions.
2.  **Kinematics:** Motion in 1D and 2D, Projectile Motion, Relative Motion.
3.  **Laws of Motion:** Newton's Laws, Friction, Circular Motion.
4.  **Work, Energy, and Power:** Work-Energy Theorem, Potential Energy, Collisions.
5.  **Rotational Motion:** Moment of Inertia, Torque, Angular Momentum, Rolling.
6.  **Gravitation:** Kepler's Laws, Gravitational Potential and Field.
7.  **Properties of Solids and Liquids:** Elasticity, Viscosity, Surface Tension, Fluid Mechanics.
8.  **Thermodynamics:** Laws of Thermodynamics, Heat Engines, Specific Heat.
9.  **Kinetic Theory of Gases:** Ideal Gas Equation, RMS Speed, Degrees of Freedom.
10. **Oscillations and Waves:** SHM, Wave Equation, Doppler Effect, Superposition.

## Class 12
11. **Electrostatics:** Coulomb's Law, Electric Field, Flux, Gauss Law, Potential.
12. **Capacitance:** Capacitors in Series/Parallel, Dielectrics.
13. **Current Electricity:** Ohm's Law, Kirchhoff's Laws, Wheatstone Bridge, Potentiometer.
14. **Magnetic Effects of Current:** Biot-Savart Law, Ampere's Law, Lorentz Force.
15. **Magnetism and Matter:** Properties of Materials, Earth's Magnetism.
16. **Electromagnetic Induction:** Faraday's Law, Lenz's Law, Inductance.
17. **Alternating Current:** LCR Circuits, Resonance, Power Factor.
18. **Electromagnetic Waves:** Displacement Current, EM Spectrum.
19. **Optics:** Ray Optics (Mirrors, Lenses, Prisms), Wave Optics (Interference, Diffraction, Polarization).
20. **Dual Nature of Matter and Radiation:** Photoelectric Effect, De-Broglie Wavelength.
21. **Atoms and Nuclei:** Bohr's Model, Radioactivity, Binding Energy.
22. **Electronic Devices:** Semiconductors, Diodes, Transistors, Logic Gates.
23. **Communication Systems:** Modulation, Signal Propagation.
