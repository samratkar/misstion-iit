# Recommended Reference Books for IIT JEE

## Physics
*   **Concepts of Physics (Vol 1 & 2)** - H.C. Verma (Foundation & Concept Building)
*   **Fundamentals of Physics** - Halliday, Resnick & Walker (Deep Understanding)
*   **Problems in General Physics** - I.E. Irodov (Advanced Problem Solving)
*   **Understanding Physics Series** - DC Pandey (Practice & JEE Specific)

## Chemistry
### Physical Chemistry
*   **Physical Chemistry** - O.P. Tandon
*   **University Chemistry** - Bruce Mahan
*   **Modern Approach to Chemical Calculations** - R.C. Mukherjee (For Mole Concept)

### Organic Chemistry
*   **Organic Chemistry** - Morrison & Boyd (Concepts)
*   **Organic Chemistry** - Solomons & Fryhle
*   **Advanced Problems in Organic Chemistry** - M.S. Chouhan

### Inorganic Chemistry
*   **Concise Inorganic Chemistry** - J.D. Lee
*   **Inorganic Chemistry** - O.P. Tandon

## Mathematics
*   **Higher Algebra** - Hall & Knight
*   **Plane Trigonometry** - S.L. Loney
*   **The Elements of Coordinate Geometry** - S.L. Loney
*   **Calculus and Analytic Geometry** - Thomas & Finney
*   **Mathematics for JEE Main & Advanced** - Cengage Series (G. Tewani)
*   **Problems in Calculus of One Variable** - I.A. Maron
